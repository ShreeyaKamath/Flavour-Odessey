# Known Limitations

- Final owned artwork is not yet included.
- Audio files are placeholder-safe and silent/generated where needed.
- AI runs through deterministic fallback/mock providers unless a future provider is configured.
- Joy Meadow is the only playable island.
- Other Version 1 islands may appear only as locked placeholders.
- Production hosting and CI deployment are documented but not fully automated in this repository.
- Screenshot slots are placeholder-ready; final image captures should be added before a public showcase submission.

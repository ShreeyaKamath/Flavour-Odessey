# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Flavor Odyssey welcomes respectful, constructive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect project scope and contributor time.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abuse.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer direction.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
